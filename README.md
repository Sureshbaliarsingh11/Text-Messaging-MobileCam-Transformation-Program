# Text-Messaging-MobileCam-Transformation-Program
Designed and implemented a Text Messaging Platform and MobileCam Solution to digitize interactions, reduce dependency on voice calls, improve response speed, and enhance customer experience. The transformation started with a controlled pilot involving 10 agents and evolved into a full-scale deployment supporting 80+ agents. 

Car rental Agency Budget Group – Digital Transformation of Emergency Roadside Services (ERS)
Role: Business Analyst / Digital Transformation Lead
Led the ideation, business analysis, pilot execution, and enterprise rollout of a digital customer experience transformation program for Car rental Agency Budget Group's Emergency Roadside Services (ERS) operation. The initiative focused on reducing agent effort, minimizing outbound call dependency, improving customer experience, and driving operational efficiency through digital channels. , 
Key Contributions
•	Conducted process analysis and opportunity mapping of the ERS vehicle replacement workflow to identify operational bottlenecks and high-effort activities. 
•	Designed and implemented a Text-as-a-Channel solution, replacing manual outbound coordination calls with structured two-way SMS communication between contact center agents, Liaison Officers, Car rental Agency locations, and customers. , 
•	Defined pilot scope, success metrics, business requirements, reporting framework, and adoption strategy for a 10-agent pilot that later scaled to 80+ agents across operations. 
•	Supported implementation of a MobileCam visual assistance solution, enabling agents to diagnose vehicle issues remotely through secure smartphone-based video sessions. , 
•	Developed the roadmap for future CRM-integrated and AI-enabled roadside assistance capabilities, including chatbot-driven self-service, visual diagnostics, OCR-enabled issue identification, and automated customer support workflows. , 
•	Established KPI dashboards and analytics to measure adoption, outbound effort reduction, response rates, and productivity improvements. 
Business Problem Solved
The ERS process relied heavily on agents making multiple outbound calls to Liaison Officers and customers to locate and authorize replacement vehicles. This resulted in high Average Handle Time (AHT), increased operational costs, slower resolutions, and customer dissatisfaction. 
Solution Delivered
•	Digital SMS communication platform with automated messaging workflows.
•	Secure agent messaging console with templates, reporting, and conversation history.
•	MobileCam visual support capability for remote vehicle diagnostics.
•	Roadmap for AI-powered ERS Bot integrated with CRM and roadside dispatch systems. , 
Business Impact
•	Achieved 29% reduction in outbound calls during pilot implementation. 
•	Delivered 32% reduction in outbound productive hours during pilot phase. 
•	Achieved 69% response rate through messaging without requiring outbound calls. 
•	Expanded solution adoption from a 10-agent pilot to 80+ FTEs across operations. 
•	Generated an average 52% reduction in outbound effort during full-scale deployment, resulting in improved productivity, lower operating costs, and enhanced customer experience.
